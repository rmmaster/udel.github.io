The Spectacled Flying Fox


A spectacled flying fox, my favorite animal!

The spectacled flying fox (pteropus conspicillatus) is notable for its fur pattern of black and light brown, especially on how the light brown fur wraps around its eyes to give it a look as if it were wearing glasses, hence their name.

I love all flying foxes, but these have always struck me as the cutest.
