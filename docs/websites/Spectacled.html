The Spectacled Flying Fox


A spectacled flying fox, my favorite animal!

The spectacled flying fox (pteropus conspicillatus) is notable for its fur pattern of black and light brown, especially on how the light brown fur wraps around its eyes to give it a look as if it were wearing glasses.

I love all flying foxes, but these have always struck me as the cutest.

For as long as I can remember, my profile picture on Steam has been a pair of two cute spectacled flying foxes. They have been my favorite animal for a long time. I would love to hold and pet one at some point in my life.
